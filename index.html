About me

I'm a Ph.D. student working in the Visual Computing group at the University of Konstanz. My area of research is information visualization, with a focus on visualization of uncertainty. I'm curious to work on challenging problems and understand their theoretical and mathematical foundations. Lately, I have been working on visualizations that help us understand machine learning algorithms.
